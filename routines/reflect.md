# Routine: reflect (monthly)

Work in the vinyl video editor repo (TTS-Vinyl-Video). Read CLAUDE.md,
`operations/health.md` history (git log), and `knowledge/decisions/`.
Then follow the `vinyl-reflect` skill: find the highest-leverage piece of
repeated friction, improve the machinery (a skill, a doctor check, a
library module), and log the change in `operations/improvements.md`.
One well-evidenced improvement beats five speculative ones.
