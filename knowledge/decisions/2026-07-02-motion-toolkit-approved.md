## Why

The plain cuts were correct but read static/slow for a social feed
(Sidney, same day). The demo proved subtle, motivated digital motion
reads as intentional editing rather than "AI edit" — the restraint caps
(<=1.2x zoom, 2-3 touches per ~20s, never on physical camera moves) are
what kept it tasteful.

## Rule?

Already codified earlier today (grammar section + skill updates); this

vinyl pull was approved twice — as a hook opener (v2) and as the finale
of the fastest variant (v3) — both times with the break of the
