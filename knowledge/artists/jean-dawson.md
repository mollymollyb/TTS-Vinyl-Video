## Guardrails (learned the hard way)

- **Never render Jean himself** — no face/likeness in generations.
  Silhouettes of generic fans/crowd are fine.
- **Audio moderation is stricter than visual**: no chants, crowd
  vocals, or anything singable in generation prompts — instrumental
  only, or render silent + add mmaudio foley. Don't ask models to
  speak/sing track titles; put titles in text overlays.
- Track titles "P4in", "Die For Me", "You're Bleeding Everywhere"
  are fine as TEXT overlays but avoid violent/self-harm IMAGERY in
  prompts (moderation + brand safety). Keep the dark-fairytale
  register aesthetic, not graphic.
- Don't fake numbers (chart stats, sales, streams) in captions.
